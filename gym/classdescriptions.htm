Class Descriptions

Splash Fit offer a wide range of classes for all age groups. Some of these are listed below:

  • Boxing

    Our main aim is to use boxing as a tool to guide young adults towards a better and healthier lifestyle. I will look to improve their knowledge of the sport and of the body as well as improving their confidence, self esteem, personal discipline, dignity and respect for themselves and towards others.

    To achieve this I will make clear the importance firstly of listening and obeying to rules and commands for example no contact means no contact anyone who hits another pupil will be removed from the class. This begins to involve the discipline and respect where I will make clear that to do boxing on a serious level the amount of dedication that is needed and required is immense and that you cannot eat junk food or drink alcohol therefore already leading towards healthier lifestyle for the pupils.
